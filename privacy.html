Last updated: March 2026  ·  This policy applies to primetimeenterprise.ltd

1. Who We Are

Prime Time Enterprise Ltd ("we", "us", "our") is a company registered in England and Wales (Company No. 08686705), with registered address at Dept 2654, 196 High Road, Wood Green, London N22 8HH.

We are the data controller for personal data collected through this website. You can contact us at office@primetimeenterprise.ltd or by calling +44 20 4587 8724.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect personal data only when you voluntarily provide it through our contact form. This includes:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Company or organisation name (if provided)
  • The content of your enquiry message

We do not collect any sensitive personal data (as defined under UK GDPR), and we do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information you submit solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry
  • Communicate with you regarding licensing, rights matters, or other services you have asked about
  • Maintain records of business correspondence

We do not use your data for marketing purposes, and we will not add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Our legal basis for processing your personal data is legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) UK GDPR) — specifically, responding to business enquiries and conducting our rights management activities. Where you are entering into a contract with us, we may also rely on contract performance (Article 6(1)(b)).

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Make (Integromat) — our contact form submissions are processed via a Make webhook to deliver enquiries to our inbox. Make processes data on our behalf as a data processor.
  • Legal obligation — where we are required to disclose data by law or court order.

6. Data Retention

We retain business correspondence, including enquiries submitted via this website, for up to 6 years in accordance with standard UK commercial practice and legal requirements. After this period, data is securely deleted.

7. International Transfers

Our website is hosted via GitHub Pages (GitHub Inc., USA). GitHub maintains appropriate safeguards for international data transfers in accordance with UK GDPR requirements. Contact form data is processed via Make, which operates within the EU/EEA under standard contractual clauses.

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Objection — object to our processing based on legitimate interests
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at office@primetimeenterprise.ltd. We will respond within one month.

9. Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies necessary for the site to function correctly. We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies. You may decline non-essential cookies via the banner shown on your first visit — however, as we use only essential cookies, declining has no effect on site functionality.

10. Complaints

If you believe we have handled your personal data unlawfully, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK's supervisory authority:

We would, however, appreciate the opportunity to address your concerns before you contact the ICO.
